Detailed Description

Component for the BydoShootingAI system. Defines the ability to shoot for a Bydo entity and the cooldown between each shot. Entity must have a TargetComponent, a PositionComponent, an InRangeComponent and (optionally), a TeamComponent (defaults to Enemy).
